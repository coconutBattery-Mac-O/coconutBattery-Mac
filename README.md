## Overview

coconutBattery Professional Suite is a complete battery management platform with history logging, alerts, and fleet monitoring for professional battery administration.

History logging records battery health snapshots over months and years, creating a degradation timeline that shows exactly when and how capacity declined.

Alerts notify when battery health drops below configurable thresholds or when unusual capacity changes indicate potential battery issues.

Fleet monitoring tracks battery health across multiple devices for organizations managing laptop and device fleets.

coconutBattery Professional Suite is the battery platform for professionals who need comprehensive battery health tracking with the history, alerts, and fleet tools that device management requires.

## Performance & Integration

coconutBattery is engineered around macOS technologies that handle performance-critical work efficiently. Tasks run in the background without slowing down the rest of your system — you can keep working while coconutBattery processes operations quietly behind the scenes.

The app integrates deeply with the macOS ecosystem out of the box:

- Launch workflows directly through **Spotlight**
- Preview files instantly with **Quick Look**
- Trigger actions from any app via the **Services menu**
- Fully compatible with **Shortcuts** for automated workflows
